Hot Cocoa recipe

Ingredients

  • Mix cocoa
  • Add milk
  • Add sugar

Steps

  1. Scoop the hot cocoa mix
  2. Heat milk and add to the mix
  3. Pour milk
  4. Stir well
  5. Add sugar
